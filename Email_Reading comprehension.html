Dear Giovanna,

Welcome to The London School of English. We are happy to send you information about your homestay accommodation.

Your host's name is Di. She is a retired doctor, and she loves gardening and theater. She lives near the school. Di is a very popular host, and former guests like talking with her in English.

Di has a daughter and 3 grandchildren, but they live in Spain. Please write to Di and briefly introduce yourself in English. Please inform her if you have any special needs.
